ASSET NOTES
Kenney.nl: Often called the "Gold Standard" of free assets. Everything is CC0, meaning you can use it for anything without even giving credit. Look for his "Pixel" or "RPG" packs.
Itch.io (Free Assets): This is a goldmine. Thousands of indie artists post free packs here. You can filter specifically for "Top-down" and "Pixel Art."
OpenGameArt.org: An older but massive community-driven site. You can find everything from character sprite sheets to environment tilesets.
CraftPix (Freebies Section): They have high-quality "Top-down" tilesets (grass, paths, towns) that would work perfectly for a Firebase multiplayer map.
